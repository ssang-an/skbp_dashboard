## 2. One-line Summary

WT-1108 is a clinically staged P2X3 chronic cough asset with validated class biology, but low SKBP CNS-theme fit and crowded competitive dynamics make it a Watch rather than Shortlist item.

## 3. Why This Asset Matters

- Strategic relevance: RCC/UCC target biology is strong, but P2X3 is mainly peripheral sensory-neuron cough biology and does not fit SKBP E/I Balance or Neuroimmune clusters.
- Scientific rationale: P2X3 antagonism is clinically validated by gefapixant Phase III/approvals and supported by camlipixant/sivopixant trials, but WT-1108-specific efficacy data are not public.
- BD relevance: Can Westan provide WT-1108-specific human Phase I/early Phase II evidence showing best-in-class efficacy, low taste disturbance, clean PK/safety and a credible global partnering path versus camlipixant/gefapixant and China P2X3 followers?
