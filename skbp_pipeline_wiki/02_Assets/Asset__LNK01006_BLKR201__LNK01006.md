## 2. One-line Summary

LNK01006 / BLKR201 is a strong SKBP Neuroimmune scientific shortlist candidate because it combines oral small-molecule modality, CNS penetration and validated TYK2 biology, but deal accessibility is constrained by Formation's ex-Greater China license and human data are still pending.

## 3. Why This Asset Matters

- Strategic relevance: TYK2 directly maps to CNS cytokine/neuroimmune biology and is positioned for MS / CNS inflammatory diseases.
- Scientific rationale: Allosteric TYK2 is clinically validated in peripheral immune disease and supported for CNS neuroinflammation, but LNK01006 has no human disease efficacy yet.
- BD relevance: Can Lynk/Formation provide quantitative human-translatable evidence that LNK01006 achieves sufficient CNS exposure and TYK2 pathway inhibition at a safe oral dose, and is any territory or collaboration structure still accessible to SKBP after the Formation/Bleecker ex-Greater China license?
