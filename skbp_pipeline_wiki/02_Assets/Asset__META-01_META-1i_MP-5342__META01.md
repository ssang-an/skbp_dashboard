## 2. One-line Summary

META-01 appears to be a historical/discontinued discovery entry, while the current practical program is MP-5342, an oral LDH inhibitor IBD PCC; SKBP CNS relevance is indirect via MS/neuroimmune expansion, so this is a Watch item rather than a shortlist asset.

## 3. Why This Asset Matters

- Strategic relevance: LDH immunometabolism has plausible Neuroimmune / peripheral immune connection relevance through pathogenic T/B-cell metabolism and MS expansion, but the disclosed lead program is IBD rather than CNS.
- Scientific rationale: LDHA/T-cell immunometabolism biology is publication-supported, but MP-5342-specific peer-reviewed compound data are not yet visible.
- BD relevance: What is the exact IP/series relationship between META-01 and MP-5342, and does MP-5342 demonstrate efficacy and safe immune-metabolic selectivity in MS/EAE or other neuroimmune-relevant models?
