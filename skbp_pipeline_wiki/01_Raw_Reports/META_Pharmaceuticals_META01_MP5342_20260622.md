## 4) Criterion Detail Pages

### 4.1 Target Relevance
Score: **2 / 3**  
Main line: **LDH immunometabolism has plausible Neuroimmune / 말초 면역기관 연결 relevance through pathogenic T/B-cell metabolism and MS expansion, but the disclosed lead program is IBD rather than CNS.**

What was checked:
- Target identity
- Disease/biology relevance
- SKBP Theme / Cluster fit
- General neurodegeneration / neuroinflammation / epilepsy relevance

Evidence trail:
- META official 2025 news states MP-5342 targets LDH, a key enzyme regulating cellular glucose metabolism, and modulates T-cell and B-cell function in inflammatory disease. [5], [6]
- META states MP-5342 has broad autoimmune potential including IBD, MS and PBC. [5], [6]
- Foundational papers show LDHA/glycolysis is important for effector T-cell differentiation and function, including Th1, CD8+ T cells and Th17 responses. [11], [12], [13]
- MS literature supports peripheral immune-cell metabolism and T-cell dysfunction as relevant to MS pathogenesis. [15], [16]

Investigation note:
- 2점인 이유는 MS 확장 가능성과 T/B-cell metabolic checkpoint라는 말초 면역-중추 연결성이 존재하기 때문이다.
- 3점이 아닌 이유는 lead indication이 IBD이고, 공개 자료에서 EAE/MS 모델, CNS 침투, CNS target engagement, microglia/astrocyte data가 보이지 않기 때문이다.
- SKBP 관점에서는 “직접 CNS asset”이라기보다 **peripheral immunometabolism으로 neuroimmune indication 확장 가능성이 있는 platform asset**에 가깝다.

Uncertain points:
- MP-5342가 MS/EAE 모델에서 효능을 보였는지 공개 자료가 부족하다.
- LDH isoform selectivity, especially LDHA vs LDHB,가 공개적으로 명확하지 않다.
- CNS exposure가 필요한 asset인지, 또는 peripheral immune modulation만으로 MS efficacy를 노리는지 불명확하다.
- META-01과 MP-5342의 정확한 lineage/IP continuity는 회사 확인 필요.

### 4.3 MoA Validity
Score: **2 / 3**  
Main line: **LDHA/T-cell immunometabolism biology는 강하지만, MP-5342 자체의 peer-reviewed compound data는 아직 공개적으로 부족하다.**

What was checked:
- Journal publication / PMID / DOI
- Mechanistic consistency
- Functional readout
- Disease linkage
- Safety-relevant signal

Evidence trail:
- Science 2016 paper reports aerobic glycolysis promotes Th1 differentiation through an epigenetic mechanism and suggests LDHA can be targeted to modulate effector T-cell differentiation. [11]
- Science 2021 paper reports LDHA is induced in CD8+ effector T cells through PI3K signaling and links glycolysis to T-cell immunity. [12]
- Immunity 2021 paper reports glycolytic ATP fuels PI3K signaling to support effector Th17 responses, relevant to autoimmune inflammation. [13]
- RA study shows inhibition of LDHA with FX11 reduced pathogenic capacity of RA CD8+ T cells to drive proinflammatory B-cell phenotype. [14]
- META/XtalPi sources claim MP-5342 has significant anti-inflammatory efficacy and >600-fold safety window, but these are not yet peer-reviewed compound-level data. [20]

Investigation note:
- 2점 이상 조건인 publication/equivalent technical evidence는 충족한다. 다만 이는 mostly target biology evidence이다.
- 3점이 아닌 이유는 MP-5342-specific in vitro potency, selectivity, in vivo IBD model efficacy, dose-response, PK/PD, safety margin의 상세 raw data가 public peer-reviewed form으로 확인되지 않았기 때문이다.

Uncertain points:
- MP-5342의 exact LDH isoform selectivity and off-target profile.
- LDH inhibition이 chronic IBD에서 systemic immunosuppression, infection risk, muscle/liver metabolic toxicity를 유발할 가능성.
- Whether MP-5342 modulates pathogenic immune cells while preserving protective immune function.
- Human translational biomarker plan: lactate, T-cell metabolic signature, cytokines, fecal calprotectin, endoscopic response.

### 4.4 Platform Attractiveness
Score: **2 / 3**  
Main line: **Oral small molecule + immunometabolism + XtalPi AI/robotics 조합은 매력적이나, platform reproducibility와 asset-level differentiation은 아직 제한적으로 공개되어 있다.**

What was checked:
- Is the platform real and reproducible?
- Is differentiation supported by data?
- Does modality fit SKBP priorities?
- Preferred modalities: small molecule, ASO, siRNA
- Secondary modalities: AOC, antibody, biologic

Evidence trail:
- META describes itself as the first China/Asia company focused on immunometabolism targets and states it has an AI-assisted metabolic pathway target discovery platform built on public datasets and disease modeling. [1], [2], [4]
- XtalPi and META collaboration is repeatedly cited in financing/news sources, including AI/FEP and robotic synthesis support for MP-5342 and META-001-PH. [6], [7], [19], [20], [31]
- XtalPi 2025 annual results state MP-5342 entered IND-enabling studies with >600-fold safety window and significant anti-inflammatory efficacy. [20]
- Oral small molecule modality fits SKBP’s preferred modality logic better than antibody-heavy IBD/TL1A competitor landscape.

Investigation note:
- 2점인 이유는 modality fit, FIC claim, XtalPi-enabled chemistry/robotics, and IND-enabling progress가 모두 확인되기 때문이다.
- 3점이 아닌 이유는 platform이 실제로 복수 asset에서 reproducible하게 성공했는지, MP-5342의 상세 selectivity/PK/efficacy data가 공개적으로 충분한지 아직 부족하기 때문이다.

Uncertain points:
- Platform이 target discovery에 어느 정도 기여했고, medicinal chemistry optimization에 어느 정도 기여했는지 구분 필요.
- MP-5342의 chemical matter novelty, patent coverage, FTO, back-up series 여부 필요.
- META-01 historical entry와 current MP-5342 program의 IP 연결성 확인 필요.

### 4.5 Expansion Potential
Score: **2 / 3**  
Main line: **IBD 외 MS/PBC/AD 등 broad autoimmune expansion 가능성은 있으나, 공개 evidence는 아직 company claim 중심이다.**

What was checked:
- Expansion beyond main indication
- Same biology/platform reuse
- Adjacent indications
- Multiple assets from same platform

Evidence trail:
- META official news states MP-5342 has broad autoimmune potential including IBD, MS and PBC. [5], [6]
- XtalPi 2025 annual results mention expansion promise into autoimmune diseases such as MS and atopic dermatitis. [20]
- META-001-PH has FDA ODD/RPDD and demonstrates a separate AI/chemistry-enabled pipeline beyond IBD, but this is a distinct PH asset and should not be treated as MP-5342 efficacy evidence. [31]
- Foundational LDHA/T-cell metabolism studies support broad immune modulation relevance, but not indication-specific clinical expansion. [11], [12], [13], [14]

Investigation note:
- 2점인 이유는 adjacent indication이 IBD 내부 subgroup이 아니라 MS, PBC, AD 등으로 넓게 제시되어 있기 때문이다.
- 3점이 아닌 이유는 각 indication별 animal model or translational biomarker data가 공개적으로 충분하지 않고, CNS/MS expansion의 경우 SKBP-relevant evidence가 아직 부족하기 때문이다.

Uncertain points:
- MS/EAE efficacy data existence.
- PBC or atopic dermatitis model data existence.
- Whether expansion uses same molecule MP-5342 or related LDH inhibitor series.
- Whether safety window remains acceptable across chronic systemic autoimmune indications.

### 4.6 Data Maturity
Score: **1 / 3**  
Main line: **PCC/IND-enabling 단계까지는 진전됐지만, 공개된 compound-level data package는 아직 부족하다.**

What was checked:
- In vitro data
- In vivo data
- Quantitative result
- Reproducibility
- IND-enabling / GLP tox / PK/PD / CMC / human data availability

Evidence trail:
- META official 2025 news says MP-5342 completed key PCC testing and is entering regulatory-required preclinical studies for IND support. [5], [6]
- VCBeat 2026 reports IND application expected H2 2026 and Phase I clinical trials thereafter. [19]
- XtalPi 2025 annual results state MP-5342 entered IND-enabling studies and demonstrated >600-fold safety window and significant anti-inflammatory efficacy. [20]
- Synapse lists MP-5342 as preclinical and LDH inhibitor, while META-01 historical entry is discovery/discontinued. [8], [9]
- No clinical trial registration or human data for MP-5342 was found as of this report.

Investigation note:
- 1점인 이유는 “PCC nominated / IND-enabling”은 확인되지만, peer-reviewed MP-5342-specific efficacy, PK/PD, toxicology, CMC, GLP package and human trial data가 아직 공개되지 않았기 때문이다.
- 만약 회사가 IBD animal model raw data, PK/PD, tox summary, and IND package를 제공하면 2점으로 상향 가능하다.

Uncertain points:
- IND-enabling tox completion status.
- GLP tox species, duration and NOAEL.
- Human FIH design and regulatory jurisdiction.
- Public clinical trial registry not yet identified.
- Whether META-01 is formally discontinued or renamed/replaced by MP-5342.

- Whether chronic LDH inhibition safety allows premium pricing and long duration.
