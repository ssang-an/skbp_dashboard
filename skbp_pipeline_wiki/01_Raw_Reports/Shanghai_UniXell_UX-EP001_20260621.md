## 4) Criterion Detail Pages

### 4.1 Target Relevance
Score: **1 / 3**

Main line:  
UX-EP001 is associated with epilepsy, which is highly relevant to SKBP’s E/I Balance theme, but the target and disease-modifying hypothesis are not disclosed for the exact asset.

What was checked:
- Target identity
- Disease/biology relevance
- SKBP Theme / Cluster fit
- General neurodegeneration / neuroinflammation / epilepsy relevance

Evidence trail:
- Synapse lists UX-EP001 as an asset from Shanghai Yuesai Biotechnology with active indication epilepsy, therapeutic area nervous system diseases, and highest phase preclinical [7].
- Synapse does not list a target, action, or mechanism for UX-EP001 [7].
- UniXell’s official epilepsy program UX-GIP001 targets drug-resistant epilepsy by differentiating iPSCs into inhibitory neuronal progenitor cells to restore GABAergic circuitry [4], [6].
- This GABAergic interneuron hypothesis strongly maps to E/I Balance and Network Modulation, but it is not safe to transfer that MoA to UX-EP001 unless the company confirms UX-EP001 = UX-GIP001 or a related program.

Investigation note:
- 1점으로 둔 이유는 epilepsy indication itself는 SKBP CNS/E-I theme relevance가 있지만, exact UX-EP001 target/MoA가 비공개이기 때문입니다.
- 2점 이상은 target or circuit mechanism이 asset-specific으로 확인될 때 가능함.

Uncertain points:
- UXEP-001이 Synapse의 UX-EP001과 동일한지 확인 필요.
- UX-EP001이 UniXell 공식 epilepsy program UX-GIP001의 구명/오타/이전 코드인지 확인 필요.
- Molecular or cellular target, delivery route, cell source, differentiation lineage가 모두 미공개.

### 4.3 MoA Validity
Score: **0 / 3**

Main line:  
UX-EP001-specific MoA evidence가 공개되어 있지 않으며, Synapse의 “TCR-T Cell therapy” 표기는 epilepsy indication과 생물학적으로 부자연스러워 검증 전까지 scoring에 사용하기 어렵습니다.

What was checked:
- Journal publication / PMID / DOI
- Mechanistic consistency
- Functional readout
- Disease linkage
- Safety-relevant signal

Evidence trail:
- Synapse lists UX-EP001 as TCR-T cell therapy, but target/action/mechanism fields are empty [7].
- No UX-EP001 publication, patent, company page, preclinical abstract, poster, registry, or IND source was found.
- UniXell’s official UX-GIP001 MoA is mechanistically coherent: impaired GABAergic interneurons drive hyperexcitability, and iPSC-derived inhibitory progenitors aim to reconstruct inhibitory circuits [4], [6].
- However, UX-GIP001 evidence cannot be used as UX-EP001-specific MoA unless asset identity is confirmed.

Investigation note:
- 0점으로 둔 이유는 exact asset에 대한 MoA가 비공개이고, 공개 데이터가 asset-specific validity를 뒷받침하지 못하기 때문입니다.
- 만약 회사가 UX-EP001 = UX-GIP001이라고 확인하면 MoA Validity는 2점까지 상향 가능성이 있습니다.

Uncertain points:
- UX-EP001의 exact modality, cellular product identity, target cell population, delivery site, mechanism, animal efficacy readout가 모두 필요합니다.
- Synapse의 TCR-T label 오류 가능성을 확인해야 합니다.

### 4.4 Platform Attractiveness
Score: **1 / 3**

Main line:  
UniXell의 hPSC/iPSC neural cell therapy platform은 공개자료상 credible하지만, UX-EP001이 이 platform에서 나온 어떤 product인지 확인되지 않습니다.

What was checked:
- Is the platform real and reproducible?
- Is differentiation supported by data?
- Does modality fit SKBP priorities?
- Preferred modalities: small molecule, ASO, siRNA
- Secondary modalities: AOC, antibody, biologic

Evidence trail:
- UniXell official page states the company has reprogramming technology, stem cell differentiation, SISBAR lineage tracing, and high-precision gene editing technologies [2], [3].
- Company source states the platform supports high-purity and batch-consistent cell products [2].
- BioSpace/PRNewswire describes 4,000 m² R&D/GMP facilities and hPSC/iPSC neural cell therapy platform capabilities [6].
- Published academic work associated with the founder/platform includes SISBAR lineage tracing and SphereDiff midbrain dopaminergic progenitor differentiation, but these publications are more directly connected to platform science and UX-DA001-type dopaminergic products, not necessarily UX-EP001 [22], [23].
- SKBP preference is generally small molecule/ASO/siRNA first; complex intracranial cell therapy is strategically interesting but outside easy-fit modality.

Investigation note:
- 1점으로 둔 이유는 platform credibility는 있으나 exact UX-EP001 differentiation lineage, CMC method, release criteria, potency assay, and safety package가 공개되어 있지 않기 때문입니다.
- Cell therapy는 CNS disease-modifying potential은 높지만, SKBP operational fit과 BD tractability는 lower than small molecule/ASO입니다.

Uncertain points:
- UX-EP001이 UniXell’s iPSC/GABA progenitor platform asset인지 불명확.
- Need cell source, allogeneic/autologous status, gene editing status, immunosuppression plan, delivery method, potency assay and release criteria.

Score: **1 / 3**

Main line:  
Company-level CNS expansion potential은 높지만, UX-EP001-specific expansion은 공개되어 있지 않습니다.

What was checked:
- Expansion beyond main indication
- Same biology/platform reuse
- Adjacent indications
- Multiple assets from same platform

Evidence trail:
- UniXell official pipeline includes multiple CNS cell therapy programs: UX-DA001/UX-DA002 for Parkinson’s disease, UX-GIP001 for epilepsy, UX-HE001 for hypoxic-ischemic encephalopathy, UX-SP001 for spinal cord injury, and undisclosed programs [3].
- Official news states UniXell will continue advancing multiple neurological cell therapy pipelines and has built both autologous and allogeneic strategies [4], [6].
- Synapse organization page lists eight drug assets and multiple neurological disease areas [5].
- UX-EP001 itself only has epilepsy listed, with no adjacent indication or platform extension data [7].

Investigation note:
- 1점으로 둔 이유는 company/platform expansion은 가능하지만 asset-specific expansion은 확인되지 않기 때문입니다.
- Adjacent indication은 same disease wording change가 아니라, MTLE 외 focal epilepsy subtype, HIE, brain injury, or other network disorders로의 확장을 의미합니다.

Uncertain points:
- UX-EP001의 product identity 확인 전까지 expansion hypothesis cannot be trusted.
- If UX-EP001 is separate from UX-GIP001, its disease scope may be narrower or entirely different.

## 6) Final Take

One-line summary:  
**UX-EP001 exact-match 기준으로는 “epilepsy preclinical asset” 외에 target/MoA/data가 공개되지 않아 Deprioritize에 가깝지만, 명칭이 UX-GIP001을 의미한 것이라면 Watch~Shortlist 재평가가 필요합니다.**

Recommendation:
- **Deprioritize for exact UX-EP001**
- **Re-check / Watch if the intended asset is UX-GIP001**

Most important diligence question:  
Is UXEP-001 a typo/alias for **UX-GIP001**, or is it a separate preclinical epilepsy asset? If separate, what is the actual modality, cell identity, target/MoA, delivery route, preclinical efficacy package, and ownership status?
