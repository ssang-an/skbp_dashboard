## 4) Criterion Detail Pages

### 4.1 Target Relevance
Score: **3 / 3**

Main line:  
LNK01006는 TYK2-mediated cytokine signaling을 CNS에서 조절하려는 oral small molecule로, SKBP Neuroimmune / Cytokine 신경조절 theme에 직접적으로 부합합니다.

What was checked:
- Target identity
- Disease/biology relevance
- SKBP Theme / Cluster fit
- General neurodegeneration / neuroinflammation / epilepsy relevance

Evidence trail:
- Lynk는 LNK01006을 **highly brain-penetrant, potent, selective oral TYK2 allosteric inhibitor targeting the JH2 domain**으로 설명하며, IL-12/IL-23 signaling, Th1/Th17 differentiation, type I interferon-related CNS inflammatory pathways를 언급했습니다 [2].
- Formation Bio는 BLKR201/LNK01006의 CNS exposure와 TYK2 selectivity가 central 또는 compartmentalized immune pathway가 관여하는 autoimmune/inflammatory diseases로 확장될 수 있다고 설명했습니다 [3], [5].
- PNAS 2025 논문은 brain-penetrant TYK2 inhibitors가 EAE/MS neuroinflammation model에서 central TYK2 inhibition의 역할을 확인했다고 보고했습니다 [11].
- Neuron23도 TYK2 loss-of-function/genetics와 MS protection, brain-penetrant TYK2 inhibition rationale을 공식 pipeline에서 설명하고 있습니다 [12].

Investigation note:
- 3점으로 평가한 이유는 target identity가 명확하고, CNS-penetrant TYK2 inhibition이 SKBP Neuroimmune theme에 직접 연결되며, MS/neuroinflammation disease biology와 human genetics / preclinical biology가 모두 visible하기 때문입니다.
- 단, 현재 임상 적응증은 아직 healthy-volunteer Phase 1이므로 disease PoC는 없습니다.

Uncertain points:
- LNK01006의 실제 CNS exposure 수치, CSF/plasma ratio, brain free fraction, species translation은 공개되지 않았습니다.
- MS 중 relapsing MS, progressive MS, PMS, AD/PD neuroinflammation 등 우선 indication은 Formation/Bleecker의 Phase 2 전략 확인이 필요합니다.

Score: **2 / 3**

Main line:  
CNS-penetrant TYK2는 아직 approved asset이 없는 differentiated white space지만, same-MoA Phase 1 competitors와 advanced MS neuroimmune competitors가 빠르게 형성되고 있습니다.

What was checked:
- Same disease competitors
- Same target competitors
- Same or similar MoA competitors
- Front runner count
- Approved / Phase 3 / clinical / preclinical status

Competitor table:

| Competitor | Company | Modality | Target / MoA | Stage | Why it matters | Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| NEU-627 | Neuron23 | Oral small molecule | Brain-penetrant TYK2 inhibitor for MS/neuroinflammation | Phase 1 listed; NEU-627 healthy-volunteer study shown as completed in Synapse/Veeva sources | Closest same-target, same-CNS-penetrant TYK2 competitor | Neuron23 [12], Synapse/Veeva [22] |
| A-005 | Alumis | Oral small molecule | CNS-penetrant allosteric TYK2 inhibitor for neuroinflammatory/neurodegenerative diseases, initial MS | Phase 1 positive data announced; development for MS/PD-like CNS indications | Direct CNS-TYK2 competitor with Phase 1 data announcement | Alumis [13] |
| Deucravacitinib / Sotyktu | Bristol Myers Squibb | Oral small molecule | Selective allosteric TYK2 inhibitor; regulatory/JH2 domain | Approved for plaque psoriasis; TYK2 clinical validation benchmark | Validates peripheral allosteric TYK2, but not CNS penetration or MS efficacy | BMS [14] |
| Zasocitinib / TAK-279 | Takeda | Oral small molecule | Highly selective allosteric TYK2 inhibitor | Phase 3 psoriasis; positive head-to-head vs deucravacitinib announced in 2026 | Shows next-gen peripheral TYK2 competition and efficacy bar for selectivity | Takeda [15], JAMA Dermatology [16] |
| Fenebrutinib | Roche | Oral small molecule | BTK inhibitor, B-cell/myeloid CNS-relevant immunomodulation | Phase 3 MS; Roche reported positive RMS/PPMS data and regulatory submission | Not same target, but major oral neuroimmune MS competitor; likely shapes MS market entry | Roche [17] |

Investigation note:
- Same target and same CNS-penetrant class were separated from broader MS competitors.
- NEU-627 and A-005 are the highest-similarity competitors because they share **brain/CNS-penetrant TYK2 inhibition**.
- Fenebrutinib/tolebrutinib are not TYK2 inhibitors but matter commercially because they are advanced oral CNS-immune MS competitors.
- Score is 2 because CNS-TYK2 is differentiated and not yet saturated, but the MS/neuroimmune field is competitive and front-runners are close or ahead.

Uncertain points:
- LNK01006 vs NEU-627 vs A-005 potency, TYK2/JAK selectivity, CNS exposure, CSF PD, safety and dose cannot be compared publicly.
- Formation/Bleecker’s exact disease indication for Phase 2 is not disclosed.
- Greater China rights remain with Lynk; global BD opportunity is already encumbered by Formation.

### 4.3 MoA Validity
Score: **2 / 3**

Main line:  
Allosteric TYK2 inhibition is clinically validated in peripheral immune disease and biologically supported in MS/neuroinflammation, but LNK01006-specific disease efficacy remains preclinical/company-reported.

What was checked:
- Journal publication / PMID / DOI
- Mechanistic consistency
- Functional readout
- Disease linkage
- Safety-relevant signal

Evidence trail:
- Deucravacitinib/Sotyktu approval validates selective allosteric TYK2 inhibition as a druggable immune target in humans [14].
- Lynk states LNK01006 targets TYK2 JH2 and modulates IL-12/IL-23, type I interferon and Th1/Th17-related inflammatory pathways relevant to CNS inflammatory diseases such as MS [2].
- PNAS 2025 central TYK2 inhibition work reported brain-penetrant TYK2 inhibitors modulated EAE/MS-like neuroinflammation and implicated astrocyte/microglial inflammatory biology [11].
- LNK01006 itself is now in first-in-human healthy-volunteer testing, but no disease-patient efficacy readout exists [5], [7].

Investigation note:
- 2점 이상으로 평가 가능한 이유는 target class, biology, genetics and preclinical CNS TYK2 rationale가 visible하기 때문입니다.
- 3점이 아닌 이유는 LNK01006-specific peer-reviewed paper, quantitative preclinical package and clinical disease PoC가 공개되지 않았기 때문입니다.

Uncertain points:
- LNK01006의 EAE model result, dose-response, biomarker modulation, free brain exposure, CSF PD data가 필요합니다.
- TYK2 inhibition이 progressive MS 또는 neurodegeneration에서 실제 clinical efficacy로 이어질지는 아직 검증되지 않았습니다.

### 4.4 Platform Attractiveness
Score: **2 / 3**

Main line:  
Oral, CNS-penetrant, selective allosteric small molecule은 SKBP 관점에서 attractive하지만, LNK01006의 best-in-class 차별성은 아직 company claim 수준입니다.

What was checked:
- Is the platform real and reproducible?
- Is differentiation supported by data?
- Does modality fit SKBP priorities?
- Preferred modalities: small molecule, ASO, siRNA
- Secondary modalities: AOC, antibody, biologic

Evidence trail:
- LNK01006은 small molecule이며, SKBP 선호 modality인 oral small molecule에 해당합니다 [2], [3].
- Formation은 LNK01006이 target selectivity, metabolic stability, CNS penetration을 위해 optimized scaffold로 설계되었다고 설명합니다 [3].
- Lynk는 JAK-STAT/TYK2 small-molecule expertise와 clinical-stage pipeline을 보유하고 있으며, 2023년 Series C funding과 2025년 Formation license deal이 external validation signal로 작동합니다 [4], [9].
- 다만 LNK01006의 chemical structure, selectivity table, kinase panel, hERG/off-target, CYP/transporters, CMC tractability, tox margin은 공개되지 않았습니다.

Investigation note:
- 2점으로 평가한 이유는 modality와 CNS-penetrant allosteric design 자체가 매력적이고 Formation deal validation이 있기 때문입니다.
- 3점은 공개 quantitative differentiation 또는 human CSF/PD data 확인 전에는 어렵습니다.

Uncertain points:
- LNK01006의 full kinase selectivity/JAK1/2/3 sparing profile 필요.
- CNS exposure가 “brain penetrant” claim을 넘어 human CSF PD로 확인되는지 필요.
- Greater China 외 rights는 Formation/Bleecker가 보유하므로 SKBP 접근 가능한 deal structure가 제한될 수 있습니다.

### 4.5 Expansion Potential
Score: **2 / 3**

Main line:  
MS에서 시작해 broader CNS inflammatory / neurodegenerative immune pathway로 확장 가능성이 있으나, 아직 Phase 1 전후 hypothesis stage입니다.

What was checked:
- Expansion beyond main indication
- Same biology/platform reuse
- Adjacent indications
- Multiple assets from same platform

Evidence trail:
- Formation은 BLKR201/LNK01006을 central or compartmentalized immune pathways가 관여하는 autoimmune/inflammatory conditions로 확장할 수 있다고 설명합니다 [3], [5].
- Synapse는 active indication으로 inflammation, multiple sclerosis, Alzheimer disease 등을 표시하고, China 쪽 AD/PD IND application status도 표시하지만, 이는 official disease-stage confirmation이 필요합니다 [8].
- PNAS central TYK2 inhibition work는 MS뿐 아니라 astrocyte/microglia-related neuroinflammation biology를 제시하며, neurodegenerative diseases로의 hypothesis 확장을 뒷받침합니다 [11].
- Formation의 AI-native development model은 indication strategy, evidence generation, trial execution을 빠르게 진행하는 플랫폼으로 포지셔닝됩니다 [5].

Investigation note:
- 2점으로 둔 이유는 biology expansion이 plausible하고 CNS penetration이 platform-like optionality를 주기 때문입니다.
- 3점은 multiple disease-specific preclinical packages 또는 clinical indication selection이 공개되어야 가능합니다.

Uncertain points:
- Formation/Bleecker가 실제로 선택한 first patient indication이 MS인지, 다른 inflammatory indication인지 불명확합니다.
- AD/PD neuroinflammation은 high-risk expansion이며, disease-modifying clinical signal 없이 시장성 반영은 제한적입니다.

### 4.6 Data Maturity
Score: **1 / 3**

Main line:  
LNK01006은 IND-cleared, first-in-human Phase 1 dosed asset이지만, public human data는 아직 없습니다.

What was checked:
- In vitro data
- In vivo data
- Quantitative result
- Reproducibility
- IND-enabling / GLP tox / PK/PD / CMC / human data availability

Evidence trail:
- FDA IND clearance for LNK01006 was announced in Dec 2025 [2].
- BLKR201-101-HV Phase 1 is a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled SAD/MAD healthy-volunteer study to evaluate safety, tolerability, PK, PD and food effect [7], [8].
- Formation/Lynk announced first participant dosing in Jun 2026 and expected readout in early 2027 [5].
- Company-reported preclinical data include high brain penetration and promising MS model efficacy, but no detailed quantitative package was found [2].
- No LNK01006-specific peer-reviewed paper, Phase 1 results, dose-exposure, CSF biomarker, tox margin or CMC package was found.

Investigation note:
- This score is driven by visible data, not deal value.
- Phase 1 dosing is a major maturity step, but no human safety/PK/PD readout exists yet, so 1점입니다.

Uncertain points:
- Need Phase 1 SAD/MAD safety, tolerability, PK, food effect and PD results.
- Need CNS exposure confirmation in human or translational CSF/brain biomarker package.
- Need IND-enabling tox and kinase selectivity package.

## 6) Final Take

One-line summary:  
**LNK01006 / BLKR201은 SKBP Neuroimmune 관점에서 매우 매력적인 CNS-penetrant allosteric TYK2 small molecule이며, scientific shortlist 가치가 있으나, Formation Bio가 Greater China 제외 권리를 이미 확보해 BD 접근성은 제한적입니다.**

Recommendation:
- **Shortlist** for scientific diligence
- BD feasibility: **Review / constrained by existing ex-Greater China license**

Most important diligence question:  
Can Lynk/Formation provide quantitative human-translatable evidence that LNK01006 achieves sufficient CNS exposure and TYK2 pathway inhibition at a safe oral dose, and is any territory or collaboration structure still accessible to SKBP after the Formation/Bleecker ex-Greater China license?
