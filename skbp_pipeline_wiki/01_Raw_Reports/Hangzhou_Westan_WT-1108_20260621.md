## 4) Criterion Detail Pages

### 4.1 Target Relevance
Score: **1 / 3**

Main line:  
P2X3 is a clinically relevant cough-hypersensitivity target, but WT-1108 does not directly map to SKBP’s E/I Balance or Neuroimmune themes.

What was checked:
- Target identity
- Disease/biology relevance
- SKBP Theme / Cluster fit
- General neurodegeneration / neuroinflammation / epilepsy relevance

Evidence trail:
- Westan product-page search evidence and Synapse identify WT-1108 as a P2X3 antagonist for RCC/UCC [2], [5].
- Mechanistic sources describe ATP-mediated P2X3 activation in airway sensory nerves as a cough-hypersensitivity pathway [16], [32].
- P2X3 is an ion-channel target, but the relevant biology is mainly peripheral sensory-neuron / airway cough reflex rather than CNS E/I Balance, epilepsy, neurodegeneration, or core neuroimmune biology.
- Pain and pruritus adjacency is mentioned in company / database sources, but no public WT-1108 clinical evidence in those indications was found [5], [8].

Investigation note:
- 1점으로 둔 이유는 respiratory / peripheral sensory indication의 target validity는 높지만, SKBP Pipeline Finder의 CNS-focused Theme / Cluster에는 직접 매핑되지 않기 때문입니다.
- 0점은 아님: P2X3는 ion channel이고 sensory-neuron pain/pruritus adjacency가 있어 peripheral neurobiology 관점의 optional relevance는 존재합니다.

Uncertain points:
- WT-1108의 CNS exposure, CNS indication strategy, neuroimmune relevance는 공개 자료에서 확인되지 않았습니다.
- Pain/pruritus expansion은 공식 임상 개발계획 또는 translational package 확인이 필요합니다.

### 4.2 Competitive Landscape
Score: **1 / 3**

Main line:  
P2X3 chronic cough class는 이미 validated 되었지만, WT-1108은 approved / Phase III / China-local Phase I-II competitors 뒤에 위치하는 late-follower risk가 큽니다.

What was checked:
- Same disease competitors
- Same target competitors
- Same or similar MoA competitors
- Front runner count
- Approved / Phase 3 / clinical / preclinical status

Competitor table:

| Competitor | Company | Modality | Target / MoA | Stage | Why it matters | Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Gefapixant / Lyfnua | Merck & Co. / MSD | Oral small molecule | P2X3 antagonist | Approved in Japan and EU-related markets; US regulatory setback / CRL history | First-in-class benchmark; validates P2X3 but highlights taste disturbance and modest-efficacy risk | EMA [15], Merck [14], Pharmaphorum [33] |
| Camlipixant / BLU-5937 | GSK / BELLUS Health | Oral small molecule | Selective P2X3 antagonist | Phase III CALM-1 / CALM-2 | Most important late-stage best-in-class competitor; GSK acquired BELLUS for ~USD 2B | GSK [17], ClinicalTrials [18], [19], PubMed [20] |
| Sivopixant / S-600918 | Shionogi | Oral small molecule | P2X3 antagonist | Phase II data published; current cough-program status uncertain | Same-MoA clinical comparator; Phase 2b efficacy was not clearly compelling | PubMed [21], Springer [22] |
| HRS-2261 | Hengrui | Oral small molecule | P2X3 antagonist | Phase I published; China Phase II registry visible; current status uncertain | China-local same-target competitor; published Phase I safety/PK benchmark | PubMed [23], Changbaicao [24], PharmCube uncertainty [34] |
| HS-10383 | Hansoh Pharma | Oral small molecule | P2X3 antagonist | Phase I positive MAD data announced; Phase II dose optimization reported | China-local once-daily competitor with no taste-related AE claim in company announcement | Hansoh [25], ClinicalTrials [26] |
| QR052107B / relicpixant | Createrna / Langlai Technology | Oral small molecule | P2X3 antagonist | Phase I positive top-line; Phase II initiated for UCC/RCC per company announcement | Another China-origin P2X3 entrant; reported no taste alteration in healthy volunteers | PRNewswire [27], Pharmacodia [28], Createrna [29] |

Investigation note:
- Same disease and same biology were checked first. The true same-MoA front runners are gefapixant and camlipixant.
- China-local competitors are especially important because WT-1108 is currently a China-origin clinical asset.
- Score is 1 because a high competitor count reduces white-space attractiveness. A higher score would require visible WT-1108 differentiation such as best-in-class taste profile, once-daily convenience, clearly superior cough-count reduction, or broader indication strategy.

Uncertain points:
- HRS-2261 active/discontinued status is unclear; a PharmCube report suggests possible exit, but official sponsor confirmation is needed [34].
- WT-1108 has no public head-to-head data versus gefapixant, camlipixant, HS-10383, HRS-2261, or QR052107B.

### 4.3 MoA Validity
Score: **2 / 3**

Main line:  
P2X3 antagonism has strong class-level clinical validation, but WT-1108-specific efficacy has not yet been publicly shown.

What was checked:
- Journal publication / PMID / DOI
- Mechanistic consistency
- Functional readout
- Disease linkage
- Safety-relevant signal

Evidence trail:
- Gefapixant COUGH-1 / COUGH-2 Phase III trials provide clinical validation for P2X3 antagonism in refractory or unexplained chronic cough [12].
- Pooled Phase III analysis also supports objective cough-frequency and patient-reported outcome improvements for gefapixant, though taste-related adverse events remain class-relevant [13].
- Merck / regulatory materials describe P2X3 receptor activity on sensory nerve fibers and ATP-mediated cough hypersensitivity [16].
- Camlipixant Phase 2b data further supports the mechanism with another selective P2X3 antagonist [20].
- WT-1108 itself has a Phase IIa PoC design with objective 24h cough-frequency endpoint, but no public efficacy readout was found [6].

Investigation note:
- 2점 이상으로 평가 가능한 이유는 publication / regulatory-grade class evidence가 존재하기 때문입니다.
- 3점이 아닌 이유는 WT-1108-specific publication, IC50/selectivity data, Phase I data, Phase IIa efficacy data가 공개되어 있지 않기 때문입니다.

Uncertain points:
- WT-1108의 P2X3 vs P2X2/3 selectivity, IC50, off-target profile, in vivo cough model 결과가 필요합니다.
- Taste disturbance risk를 낮춘다는 주장은 company / partner source 기반이며, human data로 검증되지 않았습니다 [8], [9].

### 4.4 Platform Attractiveness
Score: **1 / 3**

Main line:  
Small molecule modality는 SKBP 관점에서 다루기 좋지만, WestanAI+ 플랫폼과 WT-1108 scaffold/selectivity 차별성은 공개 근거가 약합니다.

What was checked:
- Is the platform real and reproducible?
- Is differentiation supported by data?
- Does modality fit SKBP priorities?
- Preferred modalities: small molecule, ASO, siRNA
- Secondary modalities: AOC, antibody, biologic

Evidence trail:
- ByDrug/PharmCube profile describes Westan as using proprietary compound library, AI-assisted drug discovery and rational drug design [10].
- BioDuro-Sundia/PharmCube article states WT-1108 was developed using WestanAI+, has a novel scaffold, high subtype selectivity, strong activity, high stability/bioavailability and low taste side effect potential [8].
- Zhihuiya repeats similar claims about WestanAI+ and WT-1108 differentiation [9].
- No peer-reviewed platform validation, reproducible benchmark, disclosed chemical scaffold, patent-to-data linkage, or quantitative selectivity package was found.

Investigation note:
- 1점으로 둔 이유는 modality 자체는 attractive하지만, platform differentiation이 self/partner-reported 수준에 머물기 때문입니다.
- 2점 이상을 주려면 P2X3 vs P2X2/3 selectivity, oral PK, taste-translational assay, animal cough efficacy, patent family 등 explicit data가 필요합니다.

Uncertain points:
- WestanAI+가 실제로 discovery productivity를 개선했는지 외부 검증자료가 없습니다.
- WT-1108의 exact scaffold, patent coverage, CMC tractability는 미확인입니다.

- Westan company profile suggests broader respiratory / immune-related disease focus and multiple pipeline assets [1], [10].

Investigation note:
- Adjacent indication은 단순히 “chronic cough” wording change가 아니라 RCC/UCC 외 disease context를 의미합니다.
- 현재 expansion evidence는 mostly company claim / database signal이므로 1점입니다.

Uncertain points:
- Pain/pruritus에서 P2X3 biology가 WT-1108로 충분히 translatable한지 확인 필요합니다.
- COPD/IPF cough의 regulatory path, endpoint, trial population 정의가 필요합니다.

### 4.6 Data Maturity
Score: **1 / 3**

Main line:  
Phase I completion and Phase IIa registration은 확인되지만, WT-1108-specific outcome data는 공개되어 있지 않습니다.

What was checked:
- In vitro data
- In vivo data
- Quantitative result
- Reproducibility
- IND-enabling / GLP tox / PK/PD / CMC / human data availability

Evidence trail:
- Synapse lists Phase I CTR20233488 as completed and Phase II CTR20254063 as not yet recruiting / Phase 2 [5].
- Changbaicao registry mirror describes Phase IIa design: randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, 100 mg QD, 4-week treatment, n=48, primary endpoint 24h average hourly cough frequency change at Week 4 [6].
- Sina reports Phase IIa registration / launch context, but this remains secondary news evidence [7].
- No WT-1108 Phase I safety table, PK result, food-effect result, dysgeusia result, in vitro IC50, selectivity panel, animal efficacy model, GLP tox, CMC or human efficacy readout was found.

Investigation note:
- This score is driven by visible experimental evidence, not by stage label alone.
- Phase IIa registration increases confidence that IND-enabling work occurred, but without data disclosure it does not support a 2점 maturity score.

Uncertain points:
- Need Phase I CSR/poster/publication for safety, PK and taste adverse events.
- Need preclinical pharmacology package and translational cough-model data.
- Need official ChinaDrugTrials / CDE direct confirmation for current recruitment and dosing status.

### 4.7 Marketability
Score: **1 / 3**

Main line:  
China-addressable RCC/UCC opportunity exists, but WT-1108’s obtainable peak sales is heavily reduced by class crowding, uncertain pricing and late-entry risk.

What was checked:
- Targetable addressable patient
- Unrisked peak sales
- Competition haircut
- Pricing power adjustment
- Expansion capacity adjustment

Worksheet:

| Step | What to fill | Evidence / assumption |
|---|---|---|
| A. TAP | Total Patient Pool x Diagnosis Rate x Eligibility Rate x Treatable Subgroup Rate = 7,000,000 x 45% x 50% x 70% = **1,102,500 patients** | China RCC/UCC patient pool above 7M reported by BioDuro-Sundia/PharmCube [8]. Diagnosis / eligibility / treatable subgroup rates are analyst assumptions and require payer / epidemiology validation. GSK reports 10M global and 6M US/EU RCC >1 year as burden cross-check [17]. |
| B. Unrisked Peak Sales | TAP x Annual Net Price x Peak Penetration x Treatment Duration Factor = 1,102,500 x USD 1,200 x 15% x 75% = **USD 148.8M** | Annual net price is an analyst assumption because no WT-1108 price exists. Peak penetration is capped at 15% due multiple P2X3 competitors. 75% duration factor reflects chronic therapy but tolerability / access uncertainty. |
| Entry-order matrix | 3-player example: 1st ~50%, 2nd ~30%, 3rd ~20% | WT-1108 is modeled as late follower unless it shows best-in-class taste / efficacy. Competitor count: at least 6 same-target assets in approved / Phase III / Phase I-II status [14]-[29]. |
| C. Obtainable Peak Sales | Unrisked Peak Sales x Competition Haircut x Pricing Power Adjustment x Expansion Capacity Adjustment = USD 148.8M x 45% x 90% x 120% = **USD 72.3M** | Competition haircut reflects gefapixant, camlipixant and China-local P2X3 assets. Pricing adjustment reflects uncertain China reimbursement. Expansion capacity 120% reflects unverified pruritus/pain/COPD/IPF optionality [8]. |
| Final score basis | 0 < weak market, 1 <1B obtainable, 2 >=1B, 3 >=2B + high expansion | Obtainable Peak Sales is below USD 1B, so score = 1. |

Investigation note:
- Marketability is based on obtainable peak sales, not rNPV.
- Calculation uses a conservative China-focused base because WT-1108 is a China-origin Phase IIa asset and no verified global licensing / commercial rights package was found.
- If WT-1108 obtains global rights coverage and shows best-in-class human taste/tolerability and efficacy versus camlipixant / gefapixant, obtainable peak sales could be materially higher.

Uncertain points:
- No verified WT-1108 annual net price, payer coverage or reimbursement assumption was found.
- China-specific RCC diagnosis rate and eligible moderate/severe treatable rate require epidemiology validation.
- Global market opportunity needs separate US/EU/Japan/Korea regulatory and partnering analysis.

## 6) Final Take

One-line summary:  
WT-1108은 clinically staged P2X3 chronic cough asset으로 class validation은 강하지만, SKBP CNS-theme fit이 낮고 P2X3 경쟁이 과밀하여 현재는 Shortlist보다 Watch가 적절합니다.

Recommendation:
- **Watch**

Most important diligence question:  
Can Westan provide WT-1108-specific human Phase I / early Phase II evidence showing best-in-class efficacy, low taste disturbance, clean PK/safety and a credible global partnering path versus camlipixant, gefapixant and China-local P2X3 followers?
